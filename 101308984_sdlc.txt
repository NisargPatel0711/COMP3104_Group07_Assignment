Software Development Life Cycle ( SDLC) is a method of software development, describing the steps involved in software development at each level. It covers a detailed plan for building, deploying, and maintaining the software

Prior to the 1950s, computing was not elaborate enough to necessitate a detailed approach like the SDLC.
The SDLC is not a methodology per se, but rather a description of the phases in the life cycle of a software application. In a broad sense, these phases are,:investigation, analysis, design, build, test, implement, and maintenance and support.
