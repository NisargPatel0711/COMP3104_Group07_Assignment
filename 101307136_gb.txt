George Brown College is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology with three campuses in downtown Toronto.
Produces annual rankings of the top Canadian research institutions and among colleges, for 2021 George Brown College ranked eighth in overall research income.
Also ranked fourth for the number of paid students and the number of completed projects.
Research during the 2020/21 academic year included projects that focused on improving the mental health of seniors in long-term care homes, promoting accessibility in the workforce, and empowering community members to reshape the places they live, among many others.
