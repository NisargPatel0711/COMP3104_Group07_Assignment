1. SDLC is the acronym of Software Development Life Cycle. 2 It is also called as Software Development Process.
2. SDLC is a popular practice that is followed by different organizations for designing and developing high-quality software applications.
3. SDLC has phases of the software development life cycle.
4. The first phase of SDLC is Requirements Collection.
5. The second phase of SDLC is Defining.
6. The third phase of SDLC is Designing.
