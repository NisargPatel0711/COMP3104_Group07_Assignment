Devops is my favourite subject.
DevOps speeds delivery of higher quality software by combining and automating the work of software development and IT operations teams.
DevOps is a set of practices that combines software development ( Dev) and IT operations ( Ops ).
DevOps is about connecting these worlds and eliminating the chasm that exists between the upstream and the downstream
the best DevOps processes and cultures extend beyond development and operations to incorporate inputs from all application stakeholders - including platform and infrastructure engineering, security, compliance, governance, risk management, line-of-business, end-users and customers - into the software development lifecycle.
Transitioning to DevOps requires a change in culture and mindset
